How it Works?
  1. 1. Signup to create an account.
  2. 2. After successfully signing up, Go to 'MyAccount' section to provide the details required based on if you are a NGO or an individual who is interested in donating and helping others in need.
  3. 3. Fill up your information in the 'MyAccount' & upload the required documents.
  4. 4. Once your details are verified, You are ready to go and access all the features.
  5. 5. If you are an individual and want to donate you can click on the 'Donate' button and then select the appropriate option to donate.
  6. 6. You can donate the following:
    • Money
    • Clothes
    • Medicines
    • Books
    • Services, For eg. If you are a psychologist then you can provide free service to mentor the kids of NGOs.
  7. 7. On the donate page you can filter NGOs based on their needs, Names, Location, Ratings.
  8. 8. If you are a NGO, You can choose to create a request for donation and your needs by filling required information.
